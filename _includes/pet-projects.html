Humanoid Robot Projects

  • Humanoid Robot Locomotion Simulation

    This research focuses on humanoid robot locomotion using machine learning methods, particularly in the areas of reinforcement learning and imitation learning. The goal is to develop robust locomotion policies in simulation and then transfer them to a custom-built, 3D-printed humanoid robot. This pipeline bridges the gap between simulated learning and real-world application, enabling scalable and adaptable robot control.

  • 3D Printed Humanoid Robot Project

    I've long envisioned having my own humanoid robot capable of performing real-life tasks. However, existing advanced robots—such as the Tesla Bot, Boston Dynamics’ Atlas, Unitree H and G series, and Figure AI’s robot—are either not commercially available or priced far beyond individual reach. As a result, I decided to design and build a low-cost, 3D-printable humanoid robot from scratch. This approach allows for full customization, rapid prototyping, and direct integration of algorithms tailored for real-world applications.
